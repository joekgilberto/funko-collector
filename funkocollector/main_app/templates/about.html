About Funko Collector

Funko Collector is a Django web application built by Joe Gilberto to practice working with PostgreSQL databases by interacting with data regarding a Funko Pop collection. Funko Pops are a line of collectible toys styled after many recognizable characters and people in popular culture.


For more of Joe's work, visit joekgilberto.com
